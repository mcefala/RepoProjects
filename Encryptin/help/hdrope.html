Dropbox

Encryptin can use your Dropbox account to store and retrieve encrypted files. Once you press the Dropbox button, Encryiptin will show all your Dropbox files and folders. Only the first time you will be asked to allow Encryptin to access you Dropbox account.

A right click will open a context menu from which you can choose to ENCRYPT AND UPLOAD
simply upload, DECRYPT AND DOWNLOAD
and simply download any file on your Dropbox or computer.

To upload a file, it is necessary to grant access to Encryptin to your Dropbox account.

This procedure is explained in the Settings section of this guide
