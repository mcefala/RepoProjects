Settings

From SETTINGS MENU
it is possible to grant Encrypting access to your Dropbox account:

By clicking on Permit Dropbox access you will be asked to
authorize Encryptin to access your Dropbox account in a new window.

At the end of the authorization process you will be redirected to the Dropbox homepage.

At this point we can note in the address bar of Google Chrome the "ACCESS TOKEN"
received. If the authorization is successful, some ACCOUNT
information about your Dropbox will be displayed in the Encryptin popup.

It's also possible to delete all the information stored in the browser by pressing the Delete me from this browser.

WARNING: THIS WILL DELETE ALSO THE KEY

And Logout that only erase the saved password.
